About Me

After graduating high school, I became a salesman at a local automotive tuning shop. While there, I took up welding in my spare time. Later, I worked as a manufacturing welder, and then an assembly mechanic. All the while, I held my love of sim-racing.

The very first time I encountered code was in the sim-racing title Assetto Corsa. To modify an in-game car's physics characteristics, it may be required to edit game files using a text editor. The first time I did so, I had no clue that I was coding! However, I had found my calling.

In March 2022, I began my journey to becoming a software engineer with Python4Everybody. Now, I continue on my journey as a student at Thinkful.

If you're looking to hire a new developer, please Contact Me!. I like to collaborate with other engineers and deliver great quality work.
